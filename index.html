✦ Features

Everything you need to feel supported

From reaching out to getting real-time support — SafeSpace makes guidance counseling simple and accessible.

🛡️
Anonymous & Confidential

Your reports are sent securely and only visible to your assigned guidance counselor. Your privacy is always protected.

📬
Direct Counselor Access

Reports are automatically routed to the counselor responsible for your grade level for faster, more personal support.

💬
Counselor Replies

Guidance counselors can respond directly to your concerns through the dashboard, keeping the conversation going.

🎯
Multiple Concern Types

Academic, personal, social, family — whatever you're facing, SafeSpace has a pathway to the right kind of support.

📊
Counselor Dashboard

A powerful, real-time dashboard lets counselors track, manage, and respond to student concerns with ease and care.

🔔
Status Tracking

Know when your concern has been reviewed or resolved, so you're never left wondering if your voice was heard.
